Problems in the Society

For years, the environment has slowly been decaying, slipping away from our grasp, little by little. A few noticed, some didn't.

Now is the time for urgent review.

Global surface temperature have been measured since 1880 at a network of ground-based and ocean-based sites. Over the last century, the average surface temperature of the Earth has increased by about 1⁰C. The eleven warmest years this century have all occurred since 1980, with 2016 the warmest on record.

The coastal regions are getting warmer in summers and colder in winters, as the years pass by. The average temperature margin between summers and winters is increasing everywhere. Hundreds of people are getting sick due to the sudden weather change brought about by global warming.

The earth is slowly dying and we need to save it, now.



Solution we offer

Take a step back, review the situation and let nature run through your veins till you're one with it. To make it, we need to save it. The saving starts when you decide it does.

Millions of people still believe that climate change is a hoax but climate awareness is helping raise questions. The Gen-X never had to battle climate change as an issue when they were young, unlike the Gen-Y and Gen-Z. If we ignore it now, all living species will cease to exist.

The new generation needs to be made aware of the world they will end up living in, if they don't start acting now. So, start making your mark, today. It's now or never for us.
