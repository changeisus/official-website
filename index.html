  • Timeline

    Change Is Us is a youth-led initiative co-founded by Akshat Shah and Shubh Mehta. Their journey began in 2019 and today, with over 1500+ volunteers and 80+ regular beach cleanups at Chowpatty, creating a positive impact on both the environment and society. After Akshat voiced his concerns about the environment to Shubh, they both realised that they needed to act. They approached the BMC, who arranged supplies like dustbins, ghamelas and gloves and this led to CIU’s first beach clean-up on July 21, 2019, with 18 volunteers. With BMC’s constant support since then, it has been a tumultuous journey. When the Covid-19 pandemic hit, they decided to move their work online, with webinars, social media posts to spread awareness, etc. and thus began the birth of CIU as more than just a beach cleaning group. (131 words)

  • Art for Earth and Ink for Earth

    In 2020, when the world came to a standstill, Change is Us gave art lovers and aspiring writers a platform to display their talents. Art for Earth and Ink for Earth were online events for people to express their love for the environment because Earth without art is just “Eh”. Participants were given the opportunity to win amazing all-inclusive handcrafted eco-hampers from GoKind. The creative extravaganza was judged by a very talented panel of judges. The events provided a refreshing experience as we saw various age groups interact with art of both literary and visual categories. (96 words)

  • Food and Sanitary Pad Drives

    The Covid-19 pandemic lockdown left a large number of the underprivileged population deprived of the bare necessities and hence, these food drives were carried to help those in need. Change is Us, in collaboration with the Musafir Project and Feed the Need, successfully participated in 10+ food drives, across various parts of Mumbai. Food as well as ration kits were distributed, which was made possible only because of our sponsors, crowdfunding, volunteers and awareness campaigns. Change is Us has also carried out sanitary pad distribution drives because we believe that everyone should have access to basic hygiene. They conducted 3 successful drives, distributing around 16500 pads, in all. This helped them break the stigma that surrounds feminine hygiene and spread awareness about it as well.

  • Our work till date..

    Over the 30+ months since its inception, Change is Us has conducted clean up drives for more than 80 weeks, despite the Covid-19 pandemic. We have had more than 3700 volunteers join us from various colleges, schools, spanned across different age groups in the successful collection of 145+ tonnes of waste from Girgaon Chowpatty. Our team has also conducted webinars, seminars as well as 2 online competitions to raise awareness about the environment. (73 words)
