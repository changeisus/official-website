Donation Drives

Along with beach cleaning drives, Change Is Us also collaborates and often hosts donation drives like donating- food, sanitary napkins, chocolates, clothes, etc. which are a necessity to the underprivileged sections of our society. Till date, we have distributed over 40,000 fresh cooked food boxes in the course of 135 drives, around 20,500 sanitary napkin packets and too many pieces of clothing, footwear and toys as well. Our vision is to provide the underprivileged with everything they require, from food to sanitation so that they too can have a secure future like us.Its people like us who can uplift them in any little way possible. We make a living by what we get, but we make a life with what we give.

Clean up Content

Change Is Us organises beach clean-ups every weekend at the Girgaum Chowpatty beach in Mumbai. Who doesn’t like going to beaches? But the fun is doubled when the beaches are clean and the water is clear. In the course of 79 weeks, we have cleaned over 145 tonnes of waste which included a large amount of microplastics. Once we create a mess we don’t bother cleaning up behind us, that’s where we go wrong. Our vision is to make sure we have a cleaner beach and a healthier marine ecosystem for our future generations. Become aware, make changes, take action! Mahatma Gandhi once said, “Be the change you want to see in the World!” Lets change our mindsets and the way we think for the sake of saving our oceans and beaches. Anything is better than nothing at all. Take care of the planet and the planet will take care of you.
